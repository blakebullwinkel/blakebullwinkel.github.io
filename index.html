Blake Bullwinkel

Data Scientist

I'm a Data Scientist at Microsoft and recently completed a master's at Harvard's IACS, where I was advised by Pavlos Protopapas. My thesis focused on using GANs to solve differential equations. In general, I'm interested in applied machine learning research, particularly when it overlaps with human-centered domains. I spend my free time running, practicing piano, and working on side projects.

In the spring of 2020, I earned my BA from Williams College, where I double majored in math and Chinese. I spent my junior year studying at the University of Oxford, and before my undergrad years lived in Hong Kong, Singapore, and Sydney. You can find my resume here and should feel free to reach out!
